Debut

Blackpink, the South Korean girl group formed by YG Entertainment, made their highly-anticipated debut on August 8, 2016, with the release of their single album "Square One." The group, consisting of members Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé, and Lisa, quickly captured the attention of both domestic and international audiences. Their debut single "Whistle" topped the Gaon Digital Chart, while "Boombayah," the second track from the album, became a global sensation, boasting vibrant visuals and powerful choreography. Blackpink’s unique blend of pop, hip-hop, and EDM, alongside their impressive performance prowess, set a new standard in the K-pop industry. Their debut marked the beginning of a remarkable journey that would see them grow into one of the most influential girl groups in the world, garnering a massive fanbase known as "Blinks" and paving the way for future K-pop acts on the global stage.

Members

Blackpink is a South Korean girl group formed by YG Entertainment, consisting of four dynamic members: Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé, and Lisa. Jisoo, born on January 3, 1995, serves as the main vocalist and is known for her strong stage presence and versatility. Jennie, born on January 16, 1996, is the group's rapper and vocalist, recognized for her powerful rap skills and fashion influence. Rosé, born on February 11, 1997, is the lead vocalist, captivating audiences with her unique voice and emotional delivery. Lastly, Lisa, born on March 27, 1997, is the main dancer and rapper, renowned for her exceptional dance abilities and charismatic performance style. Together, the members of Blackpink create a powerful synergy that has garnered them international recognition and a dedicated fan base, known as "Blinks." Their ability to blend various music genres, alongside their striking visuals, has solidified their position as one of the most influential K-pop groups in the world.
